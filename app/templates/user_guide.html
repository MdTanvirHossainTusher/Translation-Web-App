User Guide

Getting Started

The Healthcare Translator is designed to facilitate communication between patients and healthcare providers who speak different languages. This application converts speech to text, translates it in real-time, and offers audio playback of the translation.

How to Use

  1. Select Languages:

    Choose the patient's language from the "Patient Language" dropdown and the provider's language from the "Provider Language" dropdown. You can swap these languages using the swap button (⇄) between the dropdowns.

  2. Start Recording:

    Click the "Start Recording" button to begin capturing speech. Speak clearly into your device's microphone.

  3. View Transcription:

    As you speak, the application will transcribe your words in the "Patient Speaks" panel.

  4. Stop Recording:

    Click "Stop Recording" when you've finished speaking. The application will automatically translate your speech.

  5. View Translation:

    The translated text will appear in the "Provider Hears" panel.

  6. Audio Playback:

    Click the "Play Audio" button in either panel to hear the text spoken aloud in the respective language.

Tips for Best Results

  • Speak clearly and at a moderate pace for the best transcription accuracy.
  • Use a quiet environment to minimize background noise interference.
  • Keep sentences relatively simple and avoid complex medical jargon when possible.
  • Confirm understanding with the patient/provider after using the translator.
  • For medical emergencies, always use professional interpreters when available.

Privacy and Security

The Healthcare Translator is designed with privacy in mind:

  • All speech processing happens on your device before being sent for translation.
  • Translations are processed securely and not stored permanently.
  • No patient identifiable information should be included in the conversations.
  • This tool is not a substitute for HIPAA-compliant translation services in settings requiring such compliance.

Troubleshooting

  • Microphone not working:

    Ensure you've granted microphone permissions to your browser and that your device's microphone is functioning correctly.

  • Translation not appearing:

    Check your internet connection, as translation requires online access. Try stopping and restarting the recording.

  • Audio playback not working:

    Ensure your device's volume is turned up and that you've granted audio permissions to the browser.

Limitations

Please be aware of the following limitations:

  • While the translator is designed to handle medical terminology, it may not perfectly translate highly specialized terms.
  • The accuracy of speech recognition varies based on accent, speech clarity, and background noise.
  • This tool should be used as an aid to communication, not as the sole means of conveying critical medical information.
